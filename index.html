Jason Vale

Jason Vale, aka the ‘Juice Master’, is the best-selling author of 13 books on health, addiction and juicing. His books have collectively sold over 4 million copies, been translated into many languages and continue to inspire people from all around the world to take their health into their own hands.

Jason wasn’t always slim and healthy; he used to suffer from severe psoriasis, eczema, asthma, hay fever and obesity. He also used to smoke 2 – 3 packets of cigarettes a day and drank very heavily (as many as 14 pints of lager a day). He has used juicing as the main tool to clear his skin, drop the weight and free himself of illness. He is now on a mission to ‘Juice The World’ and encourage as many people as he can to turn their health around using natural means where possible.
